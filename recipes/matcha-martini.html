Matcha Martini

Image by user valeria_aksakova from Freepik.com

Matcha Martini, as the name suggests, is a martini cocktail with matcha, or powdered green tea, as the star ingredient. Its a creamy drink that feels like cocktail and dessert all at the same time.

Ingredients

  • 2 ounces matcha, brewed and cooled (we suggest 1 teaspoon matcha +1/4 cup hot water)
  • 1 ounce vodka
  • 1/2 ounce white creme de cacao
  • 1/2 ounce simple syrup
  • pinch of matcha powder, to garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. In a cocktail shaker filled about 3/4 with ice, add prepared matcha, vodka, creme de cacao, and simple syrup.
  3. Top with the lid, and shake for 1 to 2 minutes, or until frothy.
  4. Strain into a cocktail glass and garnish with more matcha powder, if desired.
